# Failure Patterns

## PROXY_PASS_REALITY_FAIL

Process indicators pass while the user's observable result fails.

## SELF_CERTIFIED_COMPLETION

The maker is also the only approver.

## RUBRIC_DRIFT

A failing rule is weakened or rewritten after seeing the implementation.

## STATE_COVERAGE_GAP

Only the default or low-density state is verified.

## PERCEPTUAL_BLINDNESS

Structural checks pass while the rendered or human-perceived artifact fails.

## SEMANTIC_SCOPE_CONFLICT

Individually correct values communicate incompatible scopes when combined.

## EVIDENCE_QUANTITY_FALLACY

Counts of tests, screenshots, or logs replace inspection of what they prove.

## PREMATURE_COMPLETION

Completion is declared before direct observation and independent review finish.
